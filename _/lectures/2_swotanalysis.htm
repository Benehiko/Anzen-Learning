SWOT ANALYSIS
A SWOT analysis requires the organization (or department) to evaluate two aspects of the organization's internal environment (strengths and weaknesses) and two aspects of the external environment (opportunities or threats). SWOT is an acronym for:

STRENGTHS: Characteristics of the organization that can contribute to its success.

WEAKNESSES: Characteristics of the organization that needs to improve or compensated for.

OPPORTUNITIES: Characteristics of the environment that can be (positively) exploited by the organization.

THREATS: Characteristics of the environment that can lead to negative results for the organization.
