What influences our web site information architecture?

We can distinguish between three important factors influencing information architecture:

  • The nature of our information: A four-hundred page web site will have a completely different information architecture than a four page site.
  • The nature of our users: Who they are, how many they are, how diverse they are, how much they know about our site's information, and how regular they will use the site.
  • The needs of our users: A web site is (mostly) created to fulfil a user's information need. We need to understand these needs.
